About

Welcome! My name is Son Phan. The older I got, the more I realized I was missing something. Not in a sense of material, but more abstract. After some travels with my family, I found what it was that I was missing: experience. The travel experiences I received during those family trips were highly rewarding and satisfying. This was the start of my love for travel. Since then I have made a point to see a new place every chance I got. Since then met my girlfriend who shared the same love for travel, and together we began our journey. This site will show some of my favorite travel destinations so far, with future destinations that I plan on visiting. Enjoy!
