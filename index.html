About

Welcome! My name is Son Phan. The older I got, the more I realized I was missing something. Not in a sense of material, but more abstract. After some travels with my family, I found what it was that I was missing: experience! The travel experiences I received during those family trips were highly rewarding and satisfying. This was the start of my love for travel. Since then I have made a point to see a new place every chance I got. Eventually I met my fiance who also shares the same love for travel, and so together we began our epic journey. This site will show some of my favorite travel destinations so far, along with future destinations that we plan on visiting. Enjoy and adventure on!
